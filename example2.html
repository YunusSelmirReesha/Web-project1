Formation Stages of Stars

Stages of Star Formation

  • Nebula: A cloud of gas and dust where stars are born.
  • Protostar: The gas condenses and heats up under gravity.
  • Main Sequence Star: The star enters the longest phase of its life, burning hydrogen.
  • Red Giant / Supergiant: When the hydrogen runs out, the star expands and cools.
  • Final Stage: Depending on mass, the star may become a white dwarf, neutron star, or black hole.
