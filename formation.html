Formation Stages of Stars

Birth Star

Stars begin their life in vast clouds of gas and dust known as nebulae. Gravity pulls the particles together, forming dense regions where stars can form.

Protostar

As the gas collapses, it heats up and forms a protostar. This is a very early stage where the star hasn’t yet started nuclear fusion in its core.

Main Sequence

The star begins fusing hydrogen into helium and shines steadily for millions to billions of years—this is the longest phase of a star's life.
